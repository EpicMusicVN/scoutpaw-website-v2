Option D — Cyan bg + solid pure yellow #ffd70c (aesthetic-first) AA catastrophic fail ~1.1:1
What your text request literally asks for: solid bright yellow on the current light cyan surface. Vivid. Reads as "royal yellow" unambiguously. BUT — contrast is ~1.1:1. Unreadable in sunlight, on bad monitors, for low-vision users. Documented as a known a11y exception in code-standards if chosen. Include aria-label fallbacks for screen readers.

How to use this mockup

Scroll through A–E. The toolbar at the top of the page jumps between sections. Each "stage" shows a hero + section banner rendered with the proposed colors and real-ish typography. Pick the option you actually want to ship. Reply to the brainstorm with your choice (e.g., "Go with E"). I'll then write the plan + cook.
